Movie Night

As the end of the semester approaches, students need to relax a bit and reenergize before final exams and submissions start. A fun-filled night involving some feel-good movies to freshen up

It is the lockdown again, travel restrictions persist. But hey, that doesn’t mean we can’t hang out with our friends? Although virtually, but TIE Club made sure that people don’t miss out on fun and togetherness in lockdown. What better than a movie night?!
Feel-good movies like Soul and Pursuit of Happiness gave people to come together and relax before final submissions and semester exams.
