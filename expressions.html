Expressions

We have a range of artists, from published authors to local poets, and YouTube singers to self-taught painters. There is simply no stage like Expressionism, where there is no judgment, no shaming, only support. This time we shall unite to break the stigma and open up the dialogue against sexual assault, show our support to the victims, and honour humanity by fighting depravity with our words, paint-brushes, and dance moves.
